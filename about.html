About BuzziNews

Independent journalism dedicated to covering global current affairs with clarity, speed, and context.

BuzziNews is an independent digital news outlet dedicated to covering global current affairs with clarity, speed, and context. Our mission is to provide busy readers with concise, reliable summaries and deeper reporting that helps them understand not just what happened — but why it matters.

In an age of information overload, we cut through the noise to deliver the stories that shape our world. We believe that informed citizens make better decisions, and that quality journalism is essential to democracy and progress.

Our Editorial Approach

Accuracy First

We verify facts and cite primary sources whenever possible. Every story goes through rigorous fact-checking before publication.

Concise Context

We lead with the most important facts and add context to explain the stakes. No filler, no sensationalism — just the information you need.

Transparent Sourcing

We link to official documents, statements, and original reporting. Our readers deserve to see where information comes from.

Our Commitment

Independence: We are editorially independent and committed to serving our readers, not special interests. Our funding comes from subscriptions and ethical advertising.

Diversity: We strive to represent diverse perspectives and voices in our coverage, recognizing that the issues we cover affect people differently around the world.

Accountability: We hold ourselves to the highest standards and welcome feedback. If we make mistakes, we correct them promptly and transparently.

Innovation: We embrace new storytelling formats and technologies that help us serve our audience better, while never compromising our editorial standards.
